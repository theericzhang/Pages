Take ACTion: Building a Mental Wellness Ecosystem
With the world being pinned by increasingly trying times in 2020 from COVID-19 to the impacts of systemic racism and police brutality, Jeannie Celestial and Sonia Gonzalez came to us seeking direction to help those in need of Mental Wellness.
Overview
While the world is being pressed to the edge of the seat, mental health providers are in demand more than ever. Dr. Jeannie Celestial, PhD is a licensed mental health provider at Kaiser Permanente who observed an overload of patients. With such high turnover rates and less time to engage intimately with therapists, how can patients make the most of their treatment outside of one on one sessions?
