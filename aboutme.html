About Me
Bay Area

I’m a researcher, designer, and engineer who’s dedicated to shaping engaging products.

As a self motivated end-to-end designer, I’m always on the lookout to exercise User Centered Design and design thinking on a wide breadth of products. User empathy grounds my understandings. Synthesized research intrigues me. Ideation inspires and challenges me. Testing validates my hypothesis. Held together by a strong drive to grow with my team, I see it as a priority to uplift fellow team members and learn from them.

When I have an idea, I’ll figure out a way to bring it to life.

As an enthusiastic Engineer, I’ll go the extra mile with my work. With a vast understanding of front end and back end technologies wrapped in a drive to learn even more, I have the utmost confidence in bringing a concept to life.
